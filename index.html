StartIQ

StartIQ is a startup hub for founders to organize their research, test new ideas, and connect with people.

Company Our company provides support to aspiring founders by automatically interrogating their ideas, delivering data-rich insights, organizing their artifacts, and connecting them with people.

Software We’re developing the web application with embedded javascript. Currently, running our app server on Heroku.

Founder Founded by professors at Duke University. StartIQ’s features are research backed and StartIQ team explore the interwebs to find the most relevant insights to help founders develop their ideas.
